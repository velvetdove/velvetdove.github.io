Receipt Lens

com.luckyflop.receiptlens

Scan receipts with AI, sort your spending across Gulf currencies, and calculate your Zakat — all in Arabic. The Zakat calculator tracks the lunar hawl, fetches live gold and silver prices for the nisab, and works out the 2.5%. Available as an Android app and a Chrome extension.

How we build

Three rules we don't break

Honest pricing

Clear prices and a real free tier. Nothing is designed to make you tap the wrong button or pay for something you didn't mean to.

Built for your language

Right-to-left layouts, native digits, and real translations — written in, not bolted onto an English app as an afterthought.

Your data stays yours

No bank logins, ever. Receipt photos are processed and then dropped — they're never uploaded to or stored on our servers.
